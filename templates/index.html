What does Search Cow do?

Compare prices

It minimises search time and compares the prices of a product from the top 3 e-commerce portals. It also reduces the time you spend researching about products and decreases dropout rates.

Real-time search

It refreshes the prices with each search result so that you don’t miss out on the best deals and offers on the products that you search for.

Redirects to Vendor

Once you find the deal on your product, you can instantly but it straight from the desired e-commerce portal. Just click on the buy button to be redirected.
